## Why These Credits Are Premium

### 1. High Additionality
- Wells would NOT have been plugged without carbon finance
- No regulatory mandate or public funding
- Financially prohibitive without credit revenue
- Meets all 4 Kyoto Protocol additionality tests

### 2. Permanent & Verified
- Wells permanently sealed with multi-barrier cement plugs
- Texas Railroad Commission oversight
- Third-party engineering validation
- BCarbon registry (ISO 14064 aligned)
- Full data room with 1,400+ emissions measurements

### 3. Transparency & Monitoring
- GPS trackers at every well site
- Pilot program for continuous real-time methane monitoring
- Power BI dashboard with all emissions data
- Full documentation available to buyers

### 4. Significant Co-Benefits
- 5,000+ acres of prairie restoration
- Partnership with Quail Forever & Texas Parks & Wildlife
- Biodiversity restoration (native grasses, bobwhite quail habitat)
- Water quality improvements (4 new retention ponds)
- Local job creation
- Contaminated soil remediation

### 5. Methane = High Climate Impact
- Methane is 82.5x more potent than CO2 over 20 years
- Near-term climate impact (addresses "climate emergency" narrative)
- Oil & gas sector methane reduction is a policy priority
